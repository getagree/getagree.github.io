Hi! I’m Nan Xiao, a senior data scientist working at Seven Bridges. I have a PhD degree in statistics. My research focuses on developing statistical machine learning methods to analyze high-dimensional data. My Erdős number is 4.

I’m interested in discovering the connections between things. I build software to improve my computational workflow in statistics and bioinformatics. My most popular R packages include msaenet, stackgbm, ggsci, protr, and liftr.

About

My research focuses on developing scalable statistical machine learning methods and software to solve some of the most challenging data-intensive problems in cancer genomic medicine. My Erdős number: 4.

I am an active contributor to the R and Bioconductor community, with 20+ open source R packages and Shiny applications for statistical machine learning, data visualization, and reproducible research.

I work at Seven Bridges Genomics as a data scientist, with the mission of accelerating biomedical discovery by infusing data-driven insights into cloud-based data analysis products.
